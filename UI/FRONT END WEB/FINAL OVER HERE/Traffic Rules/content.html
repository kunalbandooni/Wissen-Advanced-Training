While walking on road,

One must walk on the right side of the road.

01

While driving any vehicle,

One must stay to the left side of the road.

02

When changing lanes, or while turning left/right,

Use indicators on your vehicle. If not working, give hand signals.

03

NEVER

Overtake from left side of a vehicle.

04

ALWAYS

Give way to the ambulance, fire-fighters and other emegency services.

05

ALWAYS

Wear a helmet when on two-wheeler.

06

ALWAYS

Wear the seat-belt when sitting in your vehicle, it activates the airbag and emergency system.

07

Use Zebra Crossing to cross the road.

If you cross from anywhere else, the vehicle might not be cautious leading to accidents.

08

While moving downhill,

Always give way to the vehicles going uphill.

09

Do Not Park...

Near a road-crossing, top of a hill, near a traffic light or main road with heavy traffic.

10

Do Not Drive on a one way road except in the direction permitted.

Reversing into a one way street in the wrong direction, is also prohibited.

11

While moving downhill,

Always give way to the vehicles going uphill.

12

Use Horn in turns, and only when necessary.

Avoid using them near hospitals and schools.

13

Do Not Brake Suddenly

Except for safety reasons.

14

Do Not Carry Goods on a motor vehicle in a manner that may cause danger to any person or load.

The goods must not extend laterally beyond the side, front or to the rear of the vehicle.

15

Carry Only One Pillion Rider on your two wheeler.

You must carry the rider only on the back seat. Tripling is illegal.

16

Do Not Drive on the road if you are unwell or after taking medication

Medication that is likely to impair your driving abilities including tonics that may have an alcohol content in them.

17

Be careful when approaching parked cars or buses.

It is difficult to see people crossing from behind them. Slow down at pedestrian crossings, especially if you are turning.

18

Look Out for large turning vehicles. Do not pass them.

Your vehicle may come between the vehicle and the kerb, and may get crushed. Remember, long vehicles may use more than one lane when turning.

19

When being Overtaken, do not increase the speed.

This creates confusion for the driver trying to overtake you.

20

Use High Beam at night to see longer distance.

However, dip headlights to low beam when an approaching another vehicle.

21

Use low beam, along with dipper in foggy conditions.

High beam in fog reflects back, reducing visibility.

22

If oncoming traffic does not dip its high beam, look to the left side of the road and drive towards the left of your lane.

If you are dazzled, slow down or pull over until your eyes recover.

23

While parking to the side of road,

Make sure to put your vehicle outside of the solid white line of the road.

24

While taking a U-Turn,

Make sure to give right indicator, and look for incoming traffic.

25

When Towing a vehicle,

Driver must be there in both the vehicles, The Tower and The Towed.

26

Except for the fuel and lubricants necessary for the use of the vehicle

No explosive, highly inflammable or otherwise dangerous substance, shall be carried on any public service vehicle.

27

A driver when driving a tractor shall not carry or allow any person to be carried on tractor.
